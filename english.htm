Game tournament x Speedrunners 18:30

WHAT: Game tournament x Speedrunners

WHERE: Pii-Klubi

WHEN: The tournament begins on the 25th of April 2023 at 18:30

ENROLLMENT: From the 14th of April at noon to the 21th of April at midnight.

 

Luuppi’s video game tournament is here again! 

The game for this time's game tournament will be Speedrunners. The tournament will consist of 16 players, who will play games in groups of four. After three rounds have been played, the two people with the most points will move on to the next round of the tournament until the final four has been selected.

As usual, there are prizes for the winners.

Munkki excursion 16:00

Welcome to the munkki excursion to Pyynikki!

The traditional and idyllic cafeteria of the Pyynikki observation tower has operated as a family business for over 20 years and has become well-known for its munkki (doughnuts).

This is why we'll be walking via a 2.5 km scenic route to Pyynikki. If it is raining we can also take a bus. Dress accordingly!

Everyone will pay for their own munkki and you can see the prices here:

Menu of the cafeteria

You can also pay 2 euros to view the beautiful Pyynikki from the top of the tower.

We'll be leaving at 16:00 from front of the main building at central campus.
